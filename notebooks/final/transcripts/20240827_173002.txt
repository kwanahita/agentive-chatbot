1. **Death Benefit**: If the insured individual dies, the beneficiaries receive the higher of these three options: Benefit Amount, minimum Death Benefit, or the policy's account value.

2. **Account Value Growth**: Your premiums are invested in FWD’s professionally managed funds, with the account value changing based on market performance.

3. **Loyalty Bonus**: Every 10 years, and every 5 years after, 2% of the average account value for the past 60 months is added to the policy’s account value, provided the policy hasn't lapsed and been reinstated.

4. **Accidental Death Benefit**: An additional Benefit Amount equal to the Death Benefit is provided if death is due to an accident. This amount is tripled if the death occurs on a public holiday in the Philippines.

5. **Waiver of Premium**: If the insured becomes permanently disabled, is diagnosed with one of 42 critical illnesses, or if the owner dies (provided the owner is not the insured), all future premiums are waived. This is included in 5-pay and 10-pay plans.
